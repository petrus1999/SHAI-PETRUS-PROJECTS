ABOUT PETRUS JEWELLERS.


Petrus Jewellers in Polokwane is a subsidiary of Gold City Jewellers Company of which Petrus is the sole owner.

The group has six retail outlets (three jewellers and two specialising in a full range of luggage and leather products under the trade name, Lu Bag)


His responsibilities as general manager provided him with experience of the trade, and to fully understand and satisfy customer requirements. In addition to stocking well-known brands, Petrus Jewellers offers the design and manufacture of custom-made jewellery.

Petrus Jewellers is the most exclusive jeweller in Limpopo. High-end watch brands are stocked, and Petrus remains a leader in the jewellery trade by attending jewellery fairs locally .


Petrus Jewellers also stock long service awards, giftware, do valuations of jewellery and provide repair services. Debbie Mouton Jewellers is a member of Chevron, the largest group of independent retail jewellers in South Africa, and Petrus served as a Director on the Board for over 6 years. This gives the company access to quality suppliers, and special terms, including better purchase prices. Jewellery manufacturers, importers and agents target Chevron as a convenient way to reach the better independent retailers in Soth Africa
